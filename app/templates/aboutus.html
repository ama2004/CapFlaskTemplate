About Us

Who We Are-

We are the creators and owners of the STEM Capstone website in the Computer Science Academy.

Left: Hi, I'm Kelly. I attend Oakland Technical High School and I am a senior. I am currently studying computer science.

Middle: Hi, I'm Amanda. I'm currently a high school senior at Oakland Technical High School. I want to become a software engineer once I graduate from college.

Right: Hi, I'm Ida. I'm a senior at Oakland Tech's Computer Academy. In my free time I like playing games and baking.



Our purposes and goals:

  • to inform people who don't understand what STEM is
  • to provide resources to women and minorities from under resourced schools
  • to spread awareness about the STEM Gap
  • to encourage students to participate in STEM
